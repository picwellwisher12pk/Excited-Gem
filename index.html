Beyond Tab Management

Excited Gem is designed to revolutionize how you interact with your browser. It combines advanced session preservation with specialized media controls and secure cloud synchronization.

🚀 Smart Sessions

Save entire windows or tab groups into persistent sessions. Organize them into custom libraries to declutter your workflow instantly.

📺 YouTube Remote

Control YouTube playback (Play/Pause, Seek) directly from the extension sidebar. Full support for YouTube Shorts.

☁️ Secure Cloud Sync

Back up your workspace to your personal Google Drive. We use a Bring-Your-Own-Key (BYOK) model to ensure your data stays truly private.

🔍 Regex Search

Find specific tabs across multiple windows using powerful Regular Expression filtering on titles and URLs.
