Beyond Tab Management

Excited Gem is designed to revolutionize how you interact with your browser. It combines advanced session preservation with specialized media controls and secure cloud synchronization.

🚀 Smart Sessions

Save entire windows or tab groups into persistent sessions. Organize them into custom libraries to declutter your workspace instantly.

📺 YouTube Remote

Control YouTube playback (Play/Pause, Seek) directly from the extension sidebar. Full support for YouTube Shorts and metadata fetching for unloaded tabs.

☁️ Secure Cloud Sync

Back up your workspace to your personal Google Drive. We use a Bring-Your-Own-Key (BYOK) model so your data remains yours alone.

🔍 Regex Search

Find specific tabs across multiple windows using powerful Regular Expression filtering on titles and URLs.

🖱️ Drag & Drop

Intuitively reorder tabs and organize sessions with advanced drag-and-drop support directly in the interface.

🎨 Adaptive Layouts

Seamlessly switch between Sidebar (Side Panel), Popup, and Full Tab views to fit your browsing style.

📸 Visual Experience

Unified Workspace

A clean, real-time overview of all open tabs across all windows. Experience effortless session management and powerful media controls in a single view.
